## 💡 Pro Video Learning Tips

1. **Watch at 1.25x speed** - Saves time, still understandable
2. **Code along** - Don't just watch, actually type
3. **Take notes** - Write down key concepts
4. **Pause and practice** - Stop after each concept and try it
5. **Rewatch parts** - Don't hesitate to replay complicated sections
6. **Subscribe to channels** - Get notified of new content
7. **Download videos** - For learning offline
8. **Create playlists** - Organize by topic
